When & Where to Vote

Mark your calendar. Beaufort County Republican voters will choose their candidate on June 9, 2026. Here are all the key dates and locations.

Republican Primary
June 9, 2026
Polls open 7:00 AM – 7:00 PM
Vote at your assigned precinct
Primary Runoff
June 23, 2026
If no candidate receives a majority on June 9, top two advance to runoff
General Election
November 3, 2026
Winner of the primary faces the general election ballot
